sports_soccer How to Play Miller Family Clash 2026

flag Welcome to the Game!

Miller Family Clash 2026 is a fantasy competition for the FIFA World Cup. Compete with your family by picking winning teams and betting on matches!

groups Step 1: Pick Your 5 Teams

When you first join, you'll select 5 teams in ranked order (Rank 1 to Rank 5). Choose wisely — these picks are permanent and cannot be changed once locked!

  • Rank 1 = Your top pick (highest point multiplier)
  • Rank 5 = Your safest pick (lowest point multiplier)

💡 Strategy: Balance risky favorites with safer bets!

calculate Step 2: Earn Team Points

Your 5 teams earn Team Points based on their World Cup performance:

Team Points Formula:
For each team: Goals Scored × (Wins + Rank Bonus)
Your total = sum of all 5 teams
  • Goals: Every goal your team scores counts
  • Wins: Each match win multiplies the impact
  • Rank Bonus: Higher ranked teams get bigger multipliers (Rank 1 = 5, Rank 5 = 1)

Example: If your Rank 1 team scores 10 goals and wins 4 matches:
10 × (4 + 5) = 90 team points

toll Step 3: Earn Coins from Team Performance

Every day at midnight (PST), you automatically earn coins based on your team performance:

Coins Earned:
10% of your Team Points
  • You start with 100 coins
  • More team points = more coins earned
  • Coins are only for betting — they don't count toward your rank

casino Step 4: Bet on Matches (Optional)

Use your coins to bet on any upcoming World Cup match! Go to the Bets tab to place wagers:

  • Pick a team you think will win
  • Wager coins (default: 10 coins, adjust as you like)
  • If you win: Your wagered coins + profit convert to Bet Points and boost your rank! 🎉
  • If you lose: You just lose the coins (no points lost)

⚠️ You can cancel/delete bets anytime before the match day starts

Odds Example: Bet 50 coins on Mexico at 2.5x odds:
• Win: 50 + (50 × 1.5) = 125 points added to your total!
• Lose: -50 coins (but your team points stay safe)

emoji_events Step 5: Your Total Score & Ranking

Your leaderboard position is determined by your Total Score:

Total Score = Team Points + Bet Points
  • Team Points: Earned from your 5 team picks
  • Bet Points: Earned from successful bets

Check the Players tab to see how you stack up against everyone!

explore Navigating the App

  • Home: See upcoming matches, group standings, bracket, and the leaderboard chart
  • My Rankings: View your 5 team picks and their detailed performance stats
  • Players: Compare all players, their teams, and scores
  • Bets: Place bets on upcoming matches and view your bet history
  • Settings: Admin controls for managing the competition

lightbulb Pro Tips

  • 🔥 Diversify: Pick teams from different groups to maximize scoring opportunities
  • 💰 Bet Smart: Save coins for high-confidence bets with good odds
  • 📊 Watch Group Standings: Track which teams are advancing to knockout rounds
  • ⏰ Bet Early: Place bets before match day to lock in odds
  • 🎯 Balance Risk: Even with bad team picks, smart betting can keep you competitive!
